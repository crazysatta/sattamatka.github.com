What is Satta Matka?

Satta Matka is a popular form of kalyan satta matka that originated in India in the 1950s. It was initially based on the random selection of numbers and has since evolved into a structured game involving betting and number guessing.

The term “Matka” refers to an earthen pot used to draw numbers. Over time, the physical draw transformed into a digital number game that could be played over the phone or online.

Today, Kalyan Matka is one of the most recognized versions of the game and runs on a fixed schedule, attracting thousands of daily players.
